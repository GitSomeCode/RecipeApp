What can I do on Soupbits?

If you don't have an account, you can browse all recipes on Soupbits, and view other user's public profiles.
If you have an account, you can create and modify recipes, favorite other user's recipes, and view their public profiles.

How do I make an account?

Making an account is simple if you already have an account with Facebook, Twitter, or Google Plus. Use any of those logins, and you're good to go!

What inspired Soupbits?

Soupbits are for those who just want to keep track of the recipes they like.
Remember that time when you made that amazing apple pie, and then lost the recipe? Well not again!

What do I get if I make an account?

First of all, you must be logged in. Once logged in, you are redirected to your dashboard where you see everything related to you! That is, you can edit your profile, see all your recipes, favorited recipes, and the people who liked your recipes (including you, shameless plug!).

How do I make recipes on Soupbits?

Making a recipe requires the generic fields as well as tags. Once you tag your recipes, you can search that tag by clicking on it!

Are these all of the features on Soupbits?

  • Ability to upload pictures
  • Cool graphics
  • Cooking tips and measurements section for users
  • User shopping list
